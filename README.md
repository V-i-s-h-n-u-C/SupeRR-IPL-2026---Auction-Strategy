# 🏏 SupeRR-IPL-2026---Auction-Strategy
**Rajasthan Royals – SupeRR Selector Program**

---

## 📌 Overview  
This project was developed as part of the **SupeRR Selector Program by Rajasthan Royals**, focusing on real-world cricket analytics and decision-making.

The objective was to analyze IPL player data and build a **data-driven team selection and auction strategy** using performance metrics and interactive dashboards.

The solution integrates:
- 📊 **Power BI dashboards** for player performance analysis  
- 🧠 **Auction strategy planning** based on squad gaps, budget constraints, and player value  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement  
To design a **data-driven auction strategy for Rajasthan Royals (IPL 2026)** by:
- Identifying squad gaps  
- Evaluating players using performance metrics  
- Optimizing budget allocation  
- Building a balanced and competitive team

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source  
- **DAX** – Calculated measures and KPIs  
- **Data Modeling** – Relationship building across datasets

## 🧠 Auction Strategy (Key Highlights)  

The strategy focuses on **building a balanced squad while addressing key gaps in spin, top order, pace depth, and middle-order flexibility**.

### 🔹 Key Squad Gaps Identified  
- Lack of frontline Indian spinner  
- Left-heavy top order  
- Limited Indian wicketkeeping backup  
- Pace attack inconsistency  
- Limited all-round depth  

---

## 🎯 Target Players & Strategy  

### 1️⃣ Lead Spinner Strategy  
- 🎯 Primary Target: Ravi Bishnoi (up to ₹7 Cr)  
- 🔁 Backup: Rahul Chahar (₹6.5 Cr)  
- Goal: Control middle overs + wicket-taking  

---

### 2️⃣ Top-Order Batter  
- 🎯 Target: Prithvi Shaw (₹5 Cr)  
- 🔁 Backup: Rahul Tripathi  
- Goal: Add right-hand balance + aggressive starts  

---

### 3️⃣ Backup Wicketkeeper  
- 🎯 Target: BR Sharath (₹1 Cr)  
- Goal: Strengthen Indian bench without overseas dependency  

---

### 4️⃣ Spin Allround Depth  
- 🎯 Target: Tanush Kotian (₹1 Cr)  
- Goal: Support spin attack + lower-order batting  

---

### 5️⃣ Pace Backup  
- 🎯 Target: Sonu Yadav (₹0.5 Cr)  
- Goal: Add depth and future-ready Indian pacer  

---

### 6️⃣ Overseas Allrounder  
- 🎯 Target: Moeen Ali (₹2 Cr)  
- Alternative: Faf du Plessis (if budget allows)  
- Goal: Add flexibility, leadership, and balance  

---

## 📈 Final Strategy Outcome  

- ✅ Strong spin unit (Bishnoi/Chahar + Kotian + Jadeja)  
- ✅ Balanced top order with right-left combination  
- ✅ Improved pace depth and injury backup  
- ✅ Flexible middle order with all-round options  
- ✅ Optimized budget allocation with fallback strategies
